Discover and Speak

Improve your language skills with us

Join us as we explore the diverse world of languages and discover something new every day. Whether you're a language enthusiast or simply curious about different cultures, D&S offers a platform to connect, learn and grow together.

Our club offers opportunities to practice both Spanish and English with native speakers. Fluent speaking comes from consistent practice, and we will explain how to integrate spoken language into your daily life to improve your Spanish conversation skills. Space is limited, ask for more information to reserve a class and explore the possibility of joining our language club.

Begginer Level

Are you ready to get started with the basics of Spanish? Beginners will start with:
  • Fundamental words and phrases for everyday communication.
  • Form basics sentences to introduce themselves, ask simple questions, and engage in basic conversations.
  • Fun activities might include vocabulary games, flashcards, and simple dialogues.

Intermediate Level

  • A more extensive vocabulary, including more specific nouns, verbs, and adjectives related to daily life and common topics of interest.
  • More complex sentences and engage in longer conversations. They'll also learn to express opinions, preferences, and desires.
  • Activities can involve reading and discussing short texts, watching and discussing videos or films, and engaging in role-playing exercises.

Advanced Level

  • Delve into idiomatic expressions.
  • Activities can include advanced reading, writing essays or reports, and engaging in debates or presentations.
  • Participate in conversational groups about a specific topic.
